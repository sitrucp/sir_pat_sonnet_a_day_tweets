@SirPatStew #ASonnetADay Dashboard

Sir Patrick Stewart @SirPatStew has been doing Shakespeare Sonnet readings from his home during COVID-19 lockdown and they are really good!

They were so good I was compelled to get @SirPatStew's Twitter data and create a visualization of #ASonnetADay tweets' likes and retweet counts. I also created an interactive table with links to each tweet to allow everyone to easily watch them. Updated regularly.

Click on column header to sort asc/desc
